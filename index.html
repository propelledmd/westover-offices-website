Property Overview

A Premier Office Park Built for San Antonio's Growth

Westover Offices is a 7-building office condominium park at 9710 Westover Hills Blvd, situated in San Antonio's most dynamic commercial submarket. Each 3,000 SF building is delivered as shell-only construction, designed for medical, technology, and professional office owners.

Three buildings have already sold, validating the quality of the development and the strength of demand in Westover Hills. Buildings 1, 3, 4, and 7 remain available, a rare opportunity to own a permanent address in one of San Antonio's most sought-after commercial corridors.

  • Shell-only construction
  • O-1 zoning: medical & professional office
  • 3 per 1,000 SF dedicated parking
  • Individually owned condominium units
  • Far West Side submarket, Westover Hills
  • Minutes from major hospitals, tech employers & data centers

Strategic Location

At the Heart of San Antonio's Medical & Technology Corridor

Since its original development, Westover Hills has undergone a remarkable transformation. What began as a healthcare destination has evolved into one of South Texas's premier mixed-use corridors, anchored by a world-class medical district, a fast-growing data center sector, and a daytime workforce of over 100,000 people.

Medical District

  • CHRISTUS Santa Rosa Hospital – Westover Hills
  • Veterans Affairs (VA) Outpatient Clinic
  • Baptist Medical Center West
  • Methodist Hospital
  • Dozens of specialist medical & ancillary practices

Technology & Data

  • Major data center corridor expansion
  • Cybersecurity & healthcare IT tenants
  • Regional technology business services hub
  • Connectivity via Hwy 151 & Loop 1604
  • Growing tech & professional workforce

Key Metrics

  • 200,000 daily vehicles on Highway 151
  • 100,000+ daytime employees in the area
  • 3rd largest employment center in San Antonio
  • Direct access to Loop 1604
  • Minutes from Lackland AFB & SeaWorld
