Overview

Great America Companies, the developer of Westover Offices ("we," "our," or "us"), is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit westoveroffices.com or submit an inquiry through our contact form.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when contacting us, including:

  • Name and contact information (email address, phone number)
  • The building or property you are inquiring about
  • Any message or details you submit through our contact form

We may also collect non-personal information automatically through standard website analytics, including browser type, pages visited, time spent on the site, and referring URLs. This data is used solely to understand how visitors use the site and improve the experience.

How We Use Your Information

Information you provide is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry about available properties
  • Schedule property showings or follow-up communications
  • Send you requested information such as the property brochure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

This website may use basic cookies to support site functionality and analytics. Cookies are small text files stored on your device. You may disable cookies through your browser settings; however, some features of the site may not function as intended.

Third-Party Services

This website uses Google Fonts for typography. Google may collect limited data in accordance with its own privacy policy when fonts are loaded. We are not responsible for the data practices of third-party services.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
